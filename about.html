Background

After a long career in science, I have decided to jump over to the fast pace technology sector where I hope to have success by bridging a gap between scientist and the technology world. Much of the software currently being produced for science is arcane, and difficult to use. No matter how many people work on the software, it always seems that user experience is rarely front and center.

By taking time to learn the technical requirements for software engineering, my knowledge in the science field can be of great importance to the UX of software that is tailored to the scientific community while not compromising the rich and advanced features many come to expect from scientific software.
